Sustainable Living

Recycle

Recycling waste materials, like paper, cardboard, and plastic bottles, can save resources and prevent pollution. You can also recycle glass bottles and cans, and even some electronic devices like TVs and computer monitors.

Use Public Transport

Public transport, like buses and trains, can reduce traffic congestion, greenhouse gas emissions, and energy consumption. You can also use carpooling, ridesharing, or walk or bike to school or work.

Eat Local and Seasonal Food

Buying food that is locally or seasonally grown reduces transportation and storage energy use, carbon emissions, and waste. Additionally, it supports local farmers and businesses.
